Why this exists

Every resume you post online is a data point. Job boards, LinkedIn, recruiter portals all get scraped. That data ends up in AI training sets without your knowledge or consent.

Data poisoning is a real countermeasure. Flood the web with fake but believable data and scraped datasets become unreliable. Models trained on poisoned data perform worse. Fighting AI with AI.

Post the poisoned version wherever scrapers reach. Keep the real one for actual applications. Your resume is yours. What gets scraped is noise.
